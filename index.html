Program Experience
Prior Venture Experience 45 codes

Outcomes & Long-Term Impact
Prior Venture Experience 18 codes
  • Higher likelihood of venture continuation (80%)
  • Raised external funding (grants, investment)
  • Better positioned for scaling and growth
  • Maintained 9-year+ mentor relationships
4
First-Time Entrepreneurs 37 codes
  • Many pivoted to traditional employment
  • Retained entrepreneurial mindset in careers
  • Valued program for skills over venture outcomes
  • Long-term peer relationships and networks
  • Some felt 'not cut out' for 24/7 entrepreneurship

⚡ Key Pathway Differences

Mentor Matching: Prior venture participants needed mentors with industry-specific expertise; first-timers needed foundational guidance
Pace: Prior venture moved faster through frameworks; first-timers needed more scaffolding
Outcomes: Prior venture: venture continuation (80%); First-time: career skills and mindset (65%)
Health Impact: Both pathways showed health challenges, but first-timers reported higher stress from 'imposter syndrome'
Peer Dynamics: Prior venture participants often led peer learning; first-timers heavily relied on cohort support
